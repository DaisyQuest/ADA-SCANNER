How It Works

Simple process, expert results

From first contact to delivered report in five steps.

  1. 1

    Details

    Tell us about your product, VPAT edition, and timeline. We’ll scope and quote within 24 hours.

  2. 2

    Audit

    Comprehensive, fully manual evaluation including screen reader testing, keyboard testing, and multiple other evaluation methodologies.

  3. 3

    Remediation

    Your team fixes issues using our detailed recommendations. We’re here if you need guidance.

  4. 4

    Validation

    We verify each fix resolves the issue. If not, we provide additional direction.

  5. 5

    Documentation

    We complete your VPAT/ACR with conformance levels and detailed remarks. Ready for procurement.
